My favorite works

Title Publish date word count
Call of Cthulhu 1926 11,905 words
Herbert West-Reanimator 1921 11,998 words
The Rats in the Walls 1923 7,974 words

These three works are some of the best ever made by Lovecraft, at least in my opinion. The Call of Cthulhu his most iconic work is incredible. Herbert West is about a man and his assistant who learn to resurrect the dead. They pay the price in both sanity and blood. Rats in the Walls is about a man who discovers a hidden room related to his families past.
